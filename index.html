What is a Clause Analyzer?

A clause analyzer is a tool that helps you instantly understand legal language by identifying key risks, obligations, and phrases inside contracts. ClauseLight uses local heuristics to surface indemnity, renewal, IP, and other high-impact terms.

  • Keywords: contract analyzer, AI legal tool, analyze contracts, privacy legal AI

Why Offline Contract Analysis Matters

Most contract tools require uploading your documents to the cloud. ClauseLight is offline-first — everything runs in your browser. This eliminates privacy risks and makes it safe for confidential documents.

  • Keywords: offline AI, contract privacy, data security legal tools
