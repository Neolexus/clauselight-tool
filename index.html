What is a Clause Analyzer?

A clause analyzer is a tool that helps you instantly understand legal language by identifying key risks, obligations, and phrases inside contracts. Traditionally, lawyers would search for risks manually — but AI legal tools like ClauseLight automate this, clause-by-clause, right in your browser with no data ever leaving your device.

  • Keywords: contract analyzer, AI legal tool, analyze contracts, privacy legal AI

Why Offline Contract Analysis Matters

Most contract tools require uploading your documents to the cloud. ClauseLight is offline-first — everything runs in your browser. This eliminates data leaks and aligns with privacy laws like GDPR and POPIA. Sensitive contracts (NDA, IP, MSA) never leave your device, so you stay in control.

How ClauseLight Helps Businesses and Freelancers

Whether you’re a freelancer signing NDAs or a startup reviewing vendor contracts, ClauseLight reveals hidden terms that could impact your rights or costs.
